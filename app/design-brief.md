# 9 Birds Weekly — design brief

- Design read: internal weekly call sheet for a five-person creative studio; calm authority, zero SaaS genericism.
- Concept spine: "the call sheet" — a production run-of-show. Columns are call sheets pinned to a wall; tasks are line items ticked through the week.
- Delivery tier: editorial (internal tool; micro-motion only, no scroll theatre).
- Palette (client brand, overrides catalog bans): Warm White #F8F8F8 paper, Off-White #F0F0EE sheets, Near-Black #242422 ink, 9 Birds Blue #2E6EDD signal, Stone #E0DDD5 rules, Fog #969188 annotations. Client-color tag system for brand chips (AUTEUR bronze, CMD brick, JM green, Convi teal, MarkiBar violet, Bronco orange).
- Type: Fraunces (soft display serif, editorial masthead) + Archivo (grotesk workhorse) via Google Fonts.
- Tier-1 technique: none from catalog — internal tool; the signature is the call-sheet column with a printed-rule texture, oversized column numerals (01–04), and the tactile check interaction (stamp-in check with a paper-press micro animation).
- Sections: single screen — masthead strip, week status ribbon, four call-sheet columns, footer colophon.
- Asset plan: inline SVG "9" monogram favicon; no generated imagery (tool, not marketing page).
- CTA inventory: check chip (stamp), move menu (typewritten dropdown), refresh (rotating asterisk). Each styled individually.
